Established in 2015, I have dedicated myself to crafting captivating visual narratives defined by creativity, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to excellence in video production.

Hunter Candari is a filmmaker and creative professional who discovered his passion in a high school broadcasting class, leading to nearly a decade of competition in the 54 Hour Film Festival. He later graduated from Columbia State Community College’s film program, where he earned the Outstanding Student Award and returned as a teaching assistant. After interning at a production company and working with renowned photographer Jeremy Cowart, Hunter gained valuable experience in both creative production and building intentional, collaborative environments. Now a full-time freelancer, Hunter combines his skills and entrepreneurial spirit to craft meaningful work while maintaining the flexibility to pursue his personal and professional goals.
